About Arif Developers

Empowering businesses and individuals with cutting-edge fintech solutions since 2015. We believe in making technology accessible to everyone.

Our Story

From Kolkata to the World

Founded in 2015 by Arif Khan, Arif Developers began as a small tech startup in Kolkata with a vision to simplify financial technology for everyday users. What started as a passion project has now grown into a leading fintech solutions provider serving clients across India.

Our journey began with developing APIs for mobile recharges and money transfers. Recognizing the potential of digital financial services, we expanded our offerings to include AEPS, IRCTC ticket booking, flight reservations, PAN card services, and merchant onboarding solutions.

Today, we're proud to partner with major financial institutions and serve thousands of merchants who rely on our robust, secure, and user-friendly platforms to grow their businesses.

Our Mission

To democratize access to financial technology by creating solutions that are affordable, reliable, and easy to use for businesses and individuals across India.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted fintech partner, empowering every small business and individual with the tools they need to thrive in the digital economy.

Our Values

Innovation, Integrity, Customer Focus, Excellence, and Community. These principles guide everything we do at Arif Developers.
